## 2024-03-24 - Missing ARIA labels and focus styles on icon buttons\n**Learning:** Icon-only buttons in the web-oven app (like calendar navigation, toast dismiss, dialog closes) often miss ARIA labels, making them inaccessible to screen readers. Focus rings should also be explicitly styled against the dark slate background.\n**Action:** Add `aria-label` and `focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-orange-500` to these buttons to ensure keyboard visibility and screen reader accessibility.
