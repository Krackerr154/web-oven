## 2024-04-26 - Interactive Div Cards in Dark Themes
**Learning:** When making non-native elements like `div` cards interactive (e.g., hover/click overlays), standard visual cues aren't enough for accessibility. In dark themes, default browser focus rings often clash with background colors or are completely invisible, and screen readers won't recognize the interactivity.
**Action:** Always add `role="button"` and `tabIndex={0}` to make the element focusable and recognizable. Include an `onKeyDown` handler listening for 'Enter' and 'Space' (calling `e.preventDefault()` to stop spacebar scrolling) to trigger the same action as the click. Finally, explicitly apply custom focus styles (e.g., `focus-visible:outline-none focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-orange-500`) to guarantee focus visibility against dark backgrounds.
